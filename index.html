Pig Dice

How to play

First player rolls the die repeatedly until either:

  • 1 is rolled
  • The player chooses to hold (stop rolling)

    • If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to their score.

    First Player to score 100 points Wins!

    Good luck!
